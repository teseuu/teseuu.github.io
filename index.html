VPN Subscription Service

Concept for VPN Service

This project is made using C#, Winforms & SQL Server. Its a concept for a VPN Service named "MewVPN". Users can register or log in to their respective accounts and their information will be saved into a SQL Server. Users can subscribe to 3 different plans and get accessed to different features offered. There are also different payment methods available for the users to pay by. Demo Video Below.

×

More Info

Users will have to log in and there will be validation to check for input errors

Users will be bought to the "About Page" where they can see an overview of the software

They can edit their accounts and check their details

Afterwards, they can choose to subscribe to one of the three plans using different payment methods

Users then can turn on the VPN and select their country of choice

There is also an admin page that can be used to view and edit all user accounts and subscription
